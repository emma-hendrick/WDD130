  Right now there are a variety of factors slowing our opening, however, we plan on opening as soon as is reasonably possible, but we don't have an exact date planned. Currently we are working on perfecting the flavors we plan to serve, and setting some paperwork and liscensure.

   We will actually have a contest each month to submit a new flavor of the month. At the end of each year we will review each flavor of the month and decide if any should be moved to our permanent menu. When one of those flavors is added to the menu the person who suggested it will receive a $50 gift card for It's Truckin' Real: Rolled Ice Cream and cocao. We hope that we can get some of the community involved in choosing the flavors that they would enjoy!

  We will actually be at Porter Park at 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM on weekdays. We will be operating like a traditional ice cream truck outside of that, so we will try to drive around various residential zones as well, preferably at a time of day that won't make people angry. Over time we will come up with a more concrete route, but we do have to ask most apartments if they are ok with us coming through their parking lot, or being in a specific spot near them for a while so that people from their buildings have easy access to our ice cream. Hopefully we can get that approved so that we can bring our delicious ice cream straight to you!
